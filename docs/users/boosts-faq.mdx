## Boosts FAQ


 <h4>
    <details>
      <summary>Q: How do I integrate Snapshot to my DAO?</summary>
  
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
  
        A: You will need to insert the ENS associated with your Snapshot page when setting up the Boost. Please make sure your Snapshot space & ENS is properly set up. 
  
    </details>
  </h4>

  <h4>
    <details>
      <summary>Q: Does the Snapshot Boost equate to actual execution of voting / decision?</summary>
  
      <p>&nbsp;</p>
  
        A: No, it is more for social sentiment and consensus. Once consensus is achieved, the DAO will have to execute the decision on DAOHaus.
  
    </details>
  </h4>
