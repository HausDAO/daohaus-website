Members can stake up to 100% HAUS tokens received in monthly [DAOhaus Coordinape distribution](./overview/#participating-in-coordinape-for-haus-bonus) for shares.

**Rate**: 0.1 HAUS per share

## Steps

1. Goto HausDao Warcamp [Proposals](https://app.daohaus.club/dao/0x64/0xef3d8c4fbb1860fceab16595db7e650cd5ad51c1/proposals)
2. Click `New Proposal`
3. Select `The Classics` -> `Funding Proposal`

![funding-proposal](https://i.imgur.com/sx7pEGv.png)

4. Click `More +` - Shares Requested
5. Click `More +` - Tribute Offered

![more-funding-proposal](https://i.imgur.com/grGIMRt.png)

6. Add `TITLE`: {Name} Stake for Shares - {Month and Year}
   - **Example**: earth2travis Stake for Shares - Sept '21
7. Add `DESCRIPTION`
   - **Example**: Staking 100% of HAUS bonus from the September 2021 Coordinape epoch for shares, at 0.1 HAUS per share
8. Click `Members`
9. Select your address from the drop-down
10. Add the `SHARES REQUESTED` (Amount of HAUS tribute times 10)
11. Add the `TRIBUTE OFFERED` (Amount of HAUS tribute)
12. Click `Submit`

Next your proposal needs a sponsor and vote by the DAO.
