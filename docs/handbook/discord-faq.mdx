## Discord FAQ

<h4>
  <details>
    <summary>Q: How do I deposit funds with the Collab.Land bot?</summary>
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    A: please follow the steps below:
    <p>&nbsp;</p>
    If you already have xDAI skip to Step 4 <br />
    If you already have HAUS on xDai skip to Step 5<p>&nbsp;</p>
    <p>
      Assuming you have DAI in your MetaMask wallet on the Ethereum Mainnet...
    </p>
    <ol>
      <li>Connect your wallet on Ethereum Mainnet</li>
      <li>
        Bridge DAI to xDai using{' '}
        <a href='https://bridge.xdaichain.com/'>xDai Bridge</a>
      </li>
      <li>Connect your wallet on xDai</li>
      <li>
        Swap from XDAI to HAUS on{' '}
        <a href='https://app.honeyswap.org/#/swap'>Honeyswap</a>
      </li>
      <li>
        DM <code>!deposit 100</code> to Collab.Land bot on Discord.
        <br />
        <strong>Note:</strong> 100 is the Network Chain Id not an amount.
      </li>
      <li>
        Click link to <code>To update depositor address Click Here</code>
        <br />
        <strong>Note:</strong> If you do not update to send from the correct
        address, the tokens will be lost!
      </li>
      <li>Sign transaction in your wallet</li>
      <li>
        Send HAUS to <code>0x8dcCD27e0d1ee15e5986A1568b575B31e81F0bbA</code>
      </li>
      <li>
        DM <code>!balance</code> to Collab.Land bot to verify deposit
      </li>
      <li>Get yer tip on!</li>
    </ol>
  </details>
</h4>
