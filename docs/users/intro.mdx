Congratulations, you've found DAOhaus, the no-code platform for creating and managing a DAO.

### Who is this documentation for?

This documentation is for anybody who wants to use the [DAOhaus platform](https://app.daohaus.club) to create and manage their DAO. 

> ##### For more developer-focused documentation, head to our [Developer Documentation](/docs/devs)

### Support beyond these docs

Feel free to hit us up in our <span class='channels'>#support</span> channel on [Discord](https://discord.gg/daohaus). 
This is the best place to make feature requests, report bugs or just ask us anything about DAOs.

### Contribute
If you would like to develop a piece of content or contribute in another way, please visit [Contributing to DAOhaus](https://daohaus.club/docs/handbook/contributors/get-started).
