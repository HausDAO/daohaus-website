The DAOhaus ecosystem is mainly made up of: 
- UberHaus: The DAO of DAOs that governs the DAOhaus platform and HAUS token 
- Warcamp DAO: The development team that builds and maintain the DAOhaus platform 
- Warcamp Circles: Teams within Warcamp that work on respective workstreams

![UberHaus, Ecosystems and Circles](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/827263928882429952/870704417551302687/DAOhaus_Economy_01.png)

**Warcamp Circles**
<details>
    <summary><a href="https://daohaus.club/docs/alchemists/">Alchemists</a></summary>
    <div>
        <div>This circle is fascinated by tokenomics, providing critical insights and feedback to DAOhaus on how it can improve HAUS token functionality, and more broadly they help DAOs with their own tokenomics and microeconomies.</div>
        <br/>
    </div>
</details>

<details>
    <summary><a href="https://daohaus.club/docs/magesmiths/">Magesmiths</a></summary>
    <div>
        <div>These people are responsible for the maintaining and improving of the DAOhaus platform, think subgraphs and custom fields for interfacing with data or building in new functions for your DAO.</div>
        <br/>
    </div>
</details>

<details>
    <summary><a href="https://daohaus.club/docs/paladins/">Paladins</a></summary>
    <div>
        <div>They work to coordinate all of the circles in DAOhaus. Paladins make sure DAOhaus is on track with its scheduled deliverables as outlined in the roadmap, and they continuously provide feedback to the different circles based on information they gather.</div>
        <br/>
    </div>
</details>

<details>
    <summary><a href="https://daohaus.club/docs/rangers/">Rangers</a></summary>
    <div>
        <div>This circle is focused on growing DAOhaus and spreading the DAOspel. Rangers keep communications flowing and are dedicated to engaging with the wider community of DAOs through community events, podcasts, newsletters, and social media.</div>
    </div>
</details>
<br/>

**UberHAUS**
<details>

    <div>
